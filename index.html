About Me

I am a graduate of Bath Spa University where I completed a degree in computing. I chose this degree because of my life time passion for all things computer related, from building computers to coding and gaming I find the digital world interesting and compelling.

I find research through the internet intuitive from a young age I have taught myself skills such as: Photoshop, Blender, Unity, C++, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I've been motivated to learn these purely for enjoyment and I am able to make use of them in gaming, and also my work at redPepper Marketing where I have been a employee from 2019 to 2023 as a technical advisor and website developer whilst completing my degree.

In 2018 I established Cuber Community (https://www.youtube.com/@CubeCommunity) with a small group of like minded online friends. The aim of this community is to showcase the best Beat Saber players on a daily and consistant basis. Today the YouTube channel has 24,000+ subscribers who regularly submit content for us to showcase along side orignal content like guides and podcasts. I was an active member of the management team for this platform from launch through to 2021 helping with editing videos, creating thumbnails, managing and planning online global tournaments.
